Publisher application

This form is meant for publishers who want to be enlisted in DOAB. This information will be used to consider if the publisher is eligible for DOAB membership. If the requirements are met, this information will be presented on the DOAB website.

Please fill out the form completely (in English).

We recommend that you take these guidelines into consideration in presenting your company on your website.
